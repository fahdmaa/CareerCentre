Your Career Journey Starts Here

Welcome to EMSI Marrakech Career Center, where we've been helping engineering students launch successful careers for nearly four decades. With an 80%+ placement rate and partnerships with leading multinational companies, we're here to support every step of your professional journey.

Our Mission

As part of Morocco's leading private engineering school, our mission is simple: help every EMSI student transition seamlessly from the classroom to a thriving career. We connect talented engineering minds with top multinational companies like CGI, Capgemini, and other industry leaders, ensuring our students are ready for the challenges of tomorrow's tech landscape.

Our Vision

To maintain our position as the bridge between Morocco's top engineering talent and global opportunities, ensuring every EMSI graduate is equipped with both technical excellence and industry connections.

Industry Partnerships

Our partnerships with multinational leaders like CGI, Capgemini, HPS Worldwide, Oracle, and other tech giants provide direct pathways to exciting career opportunities for our engineering students.
